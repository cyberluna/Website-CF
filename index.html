About us

CAD Factory offers a wide range of technical services for building consultancies, especially in Rights of Light and Daylight & Sunlight. Our team was involved in some of the UK's recent landmarks, combining over two decades of experience in Rights of Light & Daylight and Sunlight. With a good wealth of knowledge in complex assessments, we consider the requirements for each individual scheme. With a proactive approach and close communication, we aim to be an extension of our client's technical department resulting in smooth data delivery which is ready to use.

3D Modelling

We specialise in producing Daylight and Sunlight /Rights of Light 3d models ready to run analysis.

ROL & DS Analsysis

Our vast experience allows us to understand and succefully complete analysis with any scenarios.

Envelopes

From maximum development envelopes (can also be combined with ROL requirements) to ROL cutbacks with development areas of loss and apportionment of the areas.

Cutbacks

From maximum development envelopes (can also be combined with ROL requirements) to ROL cutbacks with development areas of loss and apportionment of the areas.

Survey - 2D Drafting

Plans, sections and elevations produced from point cloud data.
